proportio stands for high quality formal garments, contemporary bespoke menswear. A new approach to classic masculine dresscodes, translating them into a contemporary, international and urban product.

Great care for details, fit and fabric quality in addition to the respect of fair, handmade production and great reward for the highly talented cutters and tailors, the collection offers new ways to enjoy bespoke clothing.
